PNW PLSE Workshop

Workshop on Programming Languages and Software Engineering
Research in the Pacific Northwest

The PNW PLSE workshop provides an opportunity for programming languages and software engineering researchers throughout the Pacific Northwest to meet, interact, and share work in progress as well as recent results. Meetings will feature talks and demonstrations of current projects, provide opportunities to get feedback on exciting new projects, and generally foster connections that strengthen our vibrant research community in the region.

For 2023, we'll meet at the Paul G. Allen Center at the University of Washington on Tuesday, May 9th!
